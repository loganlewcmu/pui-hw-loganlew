Visit Magens Bay and eat eat eat

OVERVIEW

Magens Bay is St. Thomas’ most-loved beach, and for good reason. Whether you need a calm morning swim, an afternoon tan, or an evening swim accompanied by a beautiful sunset view, Magens always delivers.

If you want to visit Magens but get off the beaten path, hike the Magens Bay Beach Trail.

When you’re done, grab a bite at one of our favorite local restaurants.

FOOD RECOMMENDATIONS

Udder Delight: Ice creamery with local flavors and ingredients (anything rum-flavored or rum-spiked is delicious).
Brooke’s Bar: Casual indoor-outdoor restaurant serving local staples including fish, stewed meat and veggies, plantains, and more.
Mafolie: Upscale, specializing in seafood with a beautiful view of Charlotte Amalie. Call in advance to reserve.
