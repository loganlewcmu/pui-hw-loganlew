    OVERVIEW

    St. John is the least populous island in the USVI, and home to many stunning beaches and hikes. Enjoy a relaxing ferry ride to St. John from the nearby Red Hook port.

    My family’s favorite beaches are Cinnamon Bay (white sands, tranquil water) and Trunk Bay (great for snorkeling).

    It’s also an excellent place for a sunset dinner. Just be sure to catch your ferry home!

    PLANNING TIPS

    Look at the ferry schedule and cost the day before
    Arrive at port 20 minutes before the departure time
    St John is less urban and cell service can be spotty, pack smart!
